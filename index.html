Strategy guide

Random — flip a coin each round.
Cooperate — always cooperate.
Defect — always defect.
Tit-for-Tat — mirror the partner's last move.
Unforgiving — cooperate until betrayed; defect forever.
Unknown — defaults to Tit-for-Tat.
